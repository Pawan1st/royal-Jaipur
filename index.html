About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, is known as the Pink City due to its distinctly colored buildings. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is a stunning fusion of heritage, culture, and modernity. It is home to magnificent palaces, forts, and temples that offer a glimpse into the royal past of Rajasthan.

Famous landmarks include the Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Amber Fort, and Jantar Mantar. The city's vibrant markets, exquisite handicrafts, and traditional Rajasthani cuisine add to its charm, making it a must-visit destination for travelers from around the world.
